## Background Information

On April 2, 2021 we had 307 participants. 46.9% of the participants are female, and 53.1% male. The majority of the participants (83.1%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 3.6% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 13.4% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On April 2, 2021 we had 318 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 245 participants. In 67.6% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
