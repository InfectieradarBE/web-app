## Background Information

On April 16, 2021 we had 389 participants. 50.4% of the participants are female, and 49.6% male. The majority of the participants (84.1%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 4.2% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 11.8% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On April 16, 2021 we had 958 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 365 participants. In 72,9% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
