## Background Information

On April 6, 2021 we had 355 participants. 50.7% of the participants are female, and 49.3% male. The majority of the participants (83.7%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 4.2% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 12.1% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On April 6, 2021 we had 514 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 319 participants. In 69.5% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
